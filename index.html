Welcome to The Coffee Can

Passionate about coffee...

Because we care about you, your family and our planet, we only use the very best coffee that is ethically sourced and locally roasted by the White Whale Coffee Roasters.
We only use Mungalli milk, from locally farmed cows on the Tablelands, our Syrups for coffee and frappes are from a family owned Australian company and contain no artificial colours and flavours. Our cups and lids are biodegradable and compostable with lids and lining made from plant materials not plastic and we use only paper straws.
We are passionate about coffee, supporting other local businesses and great customer service.

Why The Coffee Can?

Our Coffee Beans are roasted locally in Cairns at the White Whale Coffee Roasters. It is an exceptional blend that is consistent all year round and stands up to the local creamy Mungalli Milk that we use in all our coffees. We serve a double shot as standard and a triple in our large coffee’s. The Barista Blend we use is super smooth with notes of hazelnut & milk chocolate with a long sweet finish.
